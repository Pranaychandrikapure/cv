In short, these technological leaps have redefined the very basic of access to information and service on digital platforms since 2019 in India. With this rise in technology, there have risen issues like getting too much information, language barriers, and many other problems to access trustworthy and updated resources from the government. DocWise thus becomes the solution to such challenges: it provides a centralized multilingual platform that rethinks the way people can easily retrieve government documents and schemes. The app ensures live updates with information in multiple languages, bridges the communication gap between the government bodies and the public, making essentials available to all citizens, not only the ones who know the official languages of the government or are tech-savvy. The platform has the potential to bring about substantial betterment in the efficiency, transparency, and inclusivity of government services across the diverse population of India.
